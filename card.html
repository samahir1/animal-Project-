cats

There are over 500 million domestic cats in the world. Cats and humans have been associated for nearly 10000 years. Cats conserve energy by sleeping for an average of 13 to14 hours a day. Cats have flexible bodies and teeth adapted for hunting small animals such as mice and rats

  • Why cats? : Owning a cat can reduce the risk of stroke and heart attack by a third.
  • Facts : Your cat recognizes your voice but just acts too cool to care (probably because they are).
  • independence level : Basically, cats have a lower social IQ than dogs but can solve more difficult cognitive problems when they feel like it.
  • and last but not least : they don't like to be bossed around

Domestic cats love to play, this is especially true with kittens who love to chase toys and play fight. Play fighting among kittens may be a way for them to practice and learn skills for hunting and fighting.
